Bike Safety Statistics

According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA), bike accidents are a significant concern:

  • Fatalities: 1,105 bicyclists were killed in traffic crashes in 2022.
  • High-Risk Months: June to September see the highest number of incidents.
  • Urban Areas: Nearly 75% of bike accidents occur in urban areas.
  • Major Causes: Failure to yield, poor visibility, and distracted driving are major contributors.
×

In-Depth Statistics

Bike accident statistics reveal that:

  • Day of Week: Most accidents occur on weekends.
  • Time of Day: Evening hours are particularly risky.
  • Demographics: Young adults (ages 15-24) are at higher risk.
  • Alcohol: Alcohol involvement increases accident severity.

Safety Tips

  • Wear a helmet consistently.
  • Use reflective gear and lights at all times.
  • Follow all traffic rules and signals.
  • Be extra cautious in high-traffic areas.
