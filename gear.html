Essential Bike Safety Gear

Helmet: Always wear a properly fitted helmet. It significantly reduces the risk of head injuries in case of an accident.

Bright Clothing: Wear bright colors during the day to increase visibility. Choose neon or fluorescent colors for maximum effect.

Reflective Gear: Use reflective vests, bands, and stickers at night to ensure you're visible to drivers.

Bike Lights: Equip your bike with front and rear lights. Red lights at the back and white lights at the front are essential for night riding.

Additional Gear

Bike Bell: A bell is useful for alerting pedestrians and other cyclists of your presence, especially in crowded areas.

Mirror: Attach a rear-view mirror to keep an eye on traffic behind you without having to turn your head.
